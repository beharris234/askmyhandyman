Your Privacy
This controls whether your contacts can forward or screenshot your messages. Pick your default — you can always change it per blast or in settings.
🔒
Private — No Sharing Ever
Your blasts stay between you and your contacts. No forward button, no share link — ever. Choose this if your work requires discretion. You can always switch later.
🔓
Selective — I Decide Each Time
You get a share toggle on every blast before you send it. Turn sharing on for flyers and promos, leave it off for personal updates. Most popular — gives you full control.
📤
Open — Always Shareable
Every blast includes a share button automatically. Best for DJs and club promoters who send event flyers and want maximum reach.
