# TaxAutopilot — Outreach Templates

Copy-paste-ready scripts for reaching out to potential customers.
Each version is designed for a specific scenario.

---

## 1. WARM TEXT — Buddy you've worked with before

> Hey [name] — built something I want you to see. AI that runs the
> client side of a tax office. The wild part: free audit shows you
> $30K+ of revenue you missed last year, no card needed. Took me 5
> minutes to find $32K in my test database. Want to run it on your
> office and see your number? Link: taxautopilot.ai

clear ask, low-friction ("just see your number").

## When to use each

| Audience | Best template |
|----------|--------------|
| Close friend / longtime business buddy | #1 (SMS) or #2 (email) |
| Tax pro friend you've lost touch with | #2 (email) |
| Stranger you found online | #3 (cold email) |
| Your phone contacts | #4 (mass SMS — use a service) |
| Your social media followers | #5 (Facebook/LinkedIn post) |
| Anyone who visited the site but didn't convert | #6 (re-engagement) |
| Existing paying customer | #7 (referral ask) |

## Pro tips

1. **Personalize the [Name] field** — never blast generic. Even one
   word of personalization (their office name, their city, mutual
   friend) doubles response rates.

2. **Send between 8-10am their time** — tax pros check email
   first thing.

3. **Follow up once after 3-4 days** if no response. Most "no
   responses" are just timing.

4. **Track which templates convert** — keep a simple spreadsheet.
   After 20 sends you'll know which one works best for your network.

5. **The Money Report is your magnet** — every template leads with
   the free audit, not with "subscribe." Stick to that.
