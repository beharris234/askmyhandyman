# TaxAutopilot — 3-Minute Video Sales Letter (VSL)

Ready-to-record script. Designed for ElevenLabs voiceover + screen
recording of the actual product. ~3 minutes at conversational pace.

---

## Production notes (read this first)

- **Voice**: Use a warm, mid-range male or female voice in ElevenLabs.
  Avoid robotic-sounding voices. "Adam" or "Rachel" tend to work well
  for B2B.
- **Pace**: Conversational. Pause between sections. Aim for ~150
  words/minute.
- **Visuals**:
  - Section 1: B-roll of phone notifications buzzing, stressed person
    at desk, scattered papers
  - Section 2: Screen recording of running an actual Money Report
  - Section 3: Walk through 6 finding categories with on-screen
    numbers
  - Section 4: Calm tax pro working at a clean desk, AI dashboard
    visible
  - Section 5: Pricing card with the value stack
- **Music**: Subtle, building, professional. Royalty-free
  ("Cinematic Inspiration" or similar).
- **End frame**: TaxAutopilot logo + "taxautopilot.ai" URL +
  "Run Your Free $30K Audit →" button

## How to actually produce this

1. **Paste the script into ElevenLabs** (https://elevenlabs.io)
2. Generate the voiceover (~$0.30 in credits)
3. Open Canva (you have an account) or CapCut
4. **For visuals:**
   - Sections 1-2 (problem): Use stock B-roll from Canva's library
     ("stressed business", "phone notifications", "cluttered desk")
   - Section 3 (solution): Screen-record yourself running the actual
     Money Report on the live app
   - Sections 4-5 (proof + offer): Static cards with the dollar
     amounts and pricing
   - End: TaxAutopilot logo
5. **Add subtle background music** — Canva has a royalty-free library
6. **Export at 1080p MP4**
7. **Embed on landing page** above the Money Report section, AND
   share on LinkedIn / Facebook / Twitter

---

## Pro tips

- **Don't say "AI generated this video"** — it distracts from value
- **Show the product 60%+ of the time** — visuals of the actual
  Money Report beat any face on camera for this audience
- **Captions matter** — many people watch muted; use Canva's
  auto-captions
- **End with a 2-second logo hold** — gives viewers time to
  remember the URL
