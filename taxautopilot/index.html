Questions

Real questions from real tax pros.

Which tax software does it work with?
TaxAutopilot integrates with Drake, ProSeries, Lacerte, UltraTax, TaxWise, and TaxSlayer Pro. If you use something else, let us know — we add integrations on request and it's typically live within 2 weeks.
How long does setup take?
From signup to a fully running AI: about 5 business days. We do the setup for you — connect your software, import your clients, clean up your database, and train the AI on your office's brand and voice. You don't have to do any technical work.
Is my client data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We're SOC 2 aligned, IRS Publication 4557 compliant, and we never train AI models on your client data. Your data stays yours and is never shared.
What if my clients are older and don't want to text?
TaxAutopilot handles email, text, and phone-call transcription. Older clients keep getting emails or letters — younger clients get texts. Each client can pick how they want to be contacted, and the AI remembers it forever.
Do I lose control? Will AI text my clients without me knowing?
You're always in control. You can set the AI to "auto-send" (it handles everything itself) or "draft mode" (it writes everything, you approve before sending) — per feature. Most offices start in draft mode for the first 2 weeks, then flip to auto once they trust it.
