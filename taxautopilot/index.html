Which tax software does it work with?

Most offices save 12+ hours/week from day one.
What if my tax software isn't supported yet?
Two things: (1) the standalone tools — AI document extraction, automated client texts/emails, refund tracking, win-back campaigns, IRS notice handling — work regardless of your tax software. You still get massive value from day one. (2) Tell us what you use in the "Something Else" option above. We prioritize new integrations based on customer demand and Founders Pricing customers get a direct line to our product team to push their software up the queue.
How long does setup take?
From signup to a fully running AI: about 5 business days. We do the setup for you — connect your software, import your clients, clean up your database, and train the AI on your office's brand and voice. You don't have to do any technical work.
Is my client data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We're SOC 2 aligned, IRS Publication 4557 compliant, and we never train AI models on your client data. Your data stays yours and is never shared.
What if my clients are older and don't want to text?
TaxAutopilot handles email, text, and phone-call transcription. Older clients keep getting emails or letters — younger clients get texts. Each client can pick how they want to be contacted, and the AI remembers it forever.
Do I lose control? Will AI text my clients without me knowing?
You're always in control. You can set the AI to "auto-send" (it handles everything itself) or "draft mode" (it writes everything, you approve before sending) — per feature. Most offices start in draft mode for the first 2 weeks, then flip to auto once they trust it.
Why is annual so much cheaper than monthly?
Annual works out to about $208/month — vs $397/month on Monthly Flex. We can offer the lower rate because we know you're committed for the year, so we invest in the white-glove setup, the Money Report, and the off-season tools. Monthly Flex exists for people who want to test the water first, but most tax pros do the math and pick annual. Plus, annual locks your rate for life — monthly pricing can change at any renewal.
What's the difference between Founders Pricing and regular pricing?
Founders Pricing is $2,497/year locked for LIFE — even when we raise prices later, your rate never goes up. After we hit our first 100 offices, annual goes to $3,997/year. Founders also get priority support, the Money Left on the Table report, all off-season tools, and direct access to our product team to request features. Monthly Flex doesn't include any of these.
Can I cancel?
Anytime. No long-term contracts. Annual: if you cancel within the first 60 days and we haven't found $3,000+ of revenue opportunities in your database, you get a full refund. After 60 days you can cancel but won't be refunded for the remaining months. Monthly Flex: cancel anytime, no refund needed since you only paid for that month.
